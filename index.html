About me

Hi! I'm Rachel.

This is a site to collect miscellaneous things of mine in one place.

I work for Stripe, covering operations for Works in Progress magazine, and events for Stripe Press, a book publisher. Previously, I worked on events for Longview Philanthropy, advised the Treasury Select Committee on financial services policy, and worked a few different jobs at the Bank of England.

I've signed the Giving What We Can pledge, committing myself to giving 10% of my income to effective charity, for life. Charities I donate to include the Against Malaria Foundation, Our World In Data, the Lead Exposure Elimination Project, New Incentives, and Come Back Alive.

In my natural habitat

Theatre reviews

I occasionally review theatre in London on my substack, Rare Accidents. I've reviewed shows for Everything Theatre, Theatre Weekly, and the Reviews Hub. For older shows (2022 and back), see my archive on Wordpress.

I'm (very slowly) writing a play based on Shakespeare's Henry IV plays. An extract from The Prince's Shadow appears in The Shakespeare Multiverse, part of the Routledge Studies in Shakespeare series.
